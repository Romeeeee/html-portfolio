The Best Movies According to Rgie

My best 3 movies.

🎩 Kingsman

A stylish action spy film about a street-smart young man recruited into a secret British spy organization, where he trains to become a gentleman agent while stopping a global threat.

💔 500 Days of Summer

A non-linear romantic drama that follows Tom’s memories of his relationship with Summer, exploring love, expectations, and the difference between reality and idealized romance.

🪓 Evil Dead

A brutal horror story about a group of friends who unleash demonic forces after discovering a mysterious book in a remote cabin, leading to a terrifying fight for survival.
